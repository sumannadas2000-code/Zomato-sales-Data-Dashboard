# Zomato-sales-Data-Dashboard
Zomato sales visualisation:
This dashboard tells the story of Zomato's restaurant ecosystem by moving from market size, to city performance, to customer preferences, and finally to operational patterns.
<br>
<br>
National footprint:
<br>
The dashboard opens with a high-level view of Zomato's presence: 153 cities and 54K restaurants are covered, showing the scale of the platform's network.
<br>
<br>
City-wise restaurant concentration:
<br>
The "Restaurant by city" chart highlights where restaurant density is highest. Major metropolitan areas such as Chennai, Mumbai, Bangalore, and Pune contribute a significant share of listings, indicating stronger market penetration and customer demand in large urban centers.
<br>
<br>
Revenue and order performance:
<br>
KPI cards summarize overall business performance with approximately 691 bn total orders, 29M revenue, and an average cost of 544.19. The "Revenue by Order" trend further shows how revenue is distributed across the order volume, helping identify peaks and low-activity periods.
<br>
<br>
Customer preferences through cuisine popularity:
<br>
The cuisine analysis reveals which food categories attract the most restaurants and customer attention. Popular cuisine groups dominate the market, providing insight into consumer taste and restaurant positioning strategies.

Quality perception through ratings:
<br>
The dashboard compares restaurant ratings across cities and visualizes overall rating distribution. Most restaurants cluster within the dominant rating range, suggesting generally consistent customer satisfaction levels across the platform.
<br>
<br>
Cost vs. rating relationship:
<br>
The donut chart explores whether higher-priced restaurants necessarily receive better ratings. It helps uncover segments where affordability and customer satisfaction coexist, as well as premium segments with strong ratings.
<br>
<br>
Ordering behavior by time:
<br>
The "Orders by timings" chart identifies the hours when customers place the most orders. These peaks are useful for staffing, promotions, and delivery optimization.
<br>
<br>
<br>
Project Summary:
<br>
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Zomato restaurant data across 153 cities and 54,000+ restaurants. It combines operational metrics (orders and revenue), customer insights (ratings and cuisine popularity), and market analysis (city-wise restaurant concentration and cost vs. rating relationships). The dashboard helps identify high-performing cities, popular cuisines, customer satisfaction trends, and peak ordering periods, enabling data-driven decisions for restaurant growth and customer engagement.
